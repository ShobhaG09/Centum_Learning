Sales and Service Excellence Solution

In this new age, with digitally empowered consumers and a dynamic business environment, Sales & Service Academy is intended to help organizations grow sales, increase profit margins, enhance customer satisfaction and build strong relationships leading to brand loyalty. It is a structured system developed for organisations to develop their high-impact sales and service teams.

Employability & CSR Solutions

Organizations in the modern world, no longer work solely for profits. The focus on social responsibility has extended and it has shaped the organizational role and functioning towards the prevailing societal and environmental issues that require extended support. Centum Foundation* – the Corporate Social Responsibility company of Centum Learning, plays a pivotal role in helping design, monitor, and implement the CSR mandate of partner companies. Our CSR solutions are not merely aimed at delivering benefits but at creating a sustainable and self-running ecosystem that can bring meaningful change to lives and livelihoods. * Registered under Societies Registration Act, 1860 | Holds 80G Certificate | FCRA Registered.

Tech Enablement And Higher Education

The strategic partnership between Centum Learning and upGrad is set to revolutionize the world of B2B learning and higher education. This collaboration brings together the extensive expertise of upGrad in higher education and Centum’s rich experience in corporate training, providing businesses with a holistic lifelong learning ecosystem under one roof. In the spirit of ‘one upGrad’, our combined efforts will create a seamless and powerful learning experience for professionals and businesses alike, fostering a culture of continuous growth and development.
